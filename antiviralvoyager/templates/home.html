Welcome to Antiviral Voyager

After a natural disaster strikes, one of the most commonly overlooked aspects of the relief effort is the spread of disease. However, after a disaster has passed through an area, outbreaks can ravage a medical system that is already under heavy strain. The aim of this project is to use Machine Learning in order to predict how disease will spread after a major disaster event in order to better prepare organizations and governments for the aftermath.
